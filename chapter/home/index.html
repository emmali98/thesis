About the Author

Emma Li is an artist working in illustration, animation, and writing. Based out of Cambridge, MA, she is interested in narrative storytelling, power and fear in fantasy, and post-apocalyptic worlds. You can reach her at emma.m.li.98@gmail.com, and see other works in her portfolio.
